My KWK Journey

Data Science Scholar

June 2023

I joined Kode With Klossy as a Data Science scholar, where I explored Python, SQL, and data visualisation. It was my first hands-on experience using tech to tackle real-world issues - I built a data-driven platform addressing food insecurity in Kenya. This experience sparked my passion for data science and its potential to drive impact.

Website Development Scholar

July 2024

In 2024, I returned to KWK as a Web Development scholar, learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I developed a portfolio-building site for students navigating university applications, combining my love for education and tech to create meaningful tools for others.

Community Ambassador

Septemeber 2024 - December 2024

Later that year, I was selected as a KWK Community Ambassador. I designed digital content to spotlight underrepresented women in STEM and led initiatives to empower more girls to explore tech. This role helped me grow as both a creator and a community leader.

Instructor Assistant

May 2025 - Present

As an IA, I support scholars across three virtual Data Science camps. I lead breakout rooms, guide code-alongs, and help students bring their final projects to life. Being able to mentor and uplift scholars in the same environment where my own journey began is incredibly meaningful.

KWK X BWDC Challenge Participant

June 2025

Currently, I’m participating in the KWK x BWDC Challenge, pushing my creativity and technical skills even further. It’s a space where I can collaborate with an inspiring community and continue building tech that empowers, uplifts, and inspires change.
